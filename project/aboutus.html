Our Story

8-bit Bliss was born in a dusty attic, amidst stacks of forgotten cartridges and yellowed game manuals. Two childhood friends, Alex and Ben, rediscovered their shared passion for retro gaming while reminiscing about their favorite 8-bit adventures. They dreamed of creating a space where fellow enthusiasts could connect, share their love for classic games, and rediscover the magic of that bygone era.

Our Mission

Our mission is to celebrate the enduring legacy of 8-bit gaming. We strive to connect gamers of all ages with the joy and nostalgia of classic games. Through our website, we aim to provide a platform for sharing knowledge, discovering hidden gems, and fostering a vibrant community of retro gaming enthusiasts. We believe that the spirit of 8-bit gaming - its simplicity, creativity, and pure fun - still resonates today, and we are dedicated to keeping that spirit alive.
