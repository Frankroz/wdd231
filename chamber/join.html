NP Membership is designed for non-profit organizations. It is a free membership that provides access to networking opportunities, community resource sharing, and inclusion in our online directory. This membership is ideal for organizations looking to connect with the local business community and expand their reach.

×

Bronze Membership is an entry-level paid membership. It includes all the benefits of NP Membership, plus access to exclusive training workshops, discounted event tickets, and enhanced listing in our online directory. This level is perfect for small businesses and startups looking to grow their network.

×

Silver Membership offers increased visibility and networking opportunities. Members receive all Bronze benefits, plus priority listing in the directory, advertising opportunities on our website, and special invitations to VIP events. This level is tailored for established businesses seeking to maximize their exposure.

×

Gold Membership is our premium level, providing the highest level of benefits. Members enjoy all Silver benefits, along with prominent spotlight positions on our homepage, exclusive event sponsorships, and personalized business consultations. This membership is ideal for large corporations and organizations aiming for maximum impact.
